When searching for care, look for those who...

  • Enjoy working with young children.
  • Give young children plenty of individual attention.
  • Understand how young children learn.
  • Speak gently to children.
  • Establish routines for activities.
  • Are responsive to parents' concerns about their children.
  • Accept and discipline children through helping, teaching, and learning.

Also look for...

  • Clean, pleasant surroundings.
  • Nutritious meals and snacks that are varied, attractive, and served at appropriate times.
  • A balance of active and quiet play times.
  • Age-appropriate toys, books, art and music materials.
  • Indoor and outdoor play areas that are safe and secure, and promote a child's urge to explore.
